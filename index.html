Ear Training

To install this app from the Google Play Store, click here.

This android app is designed to help music students prepare for the ear tests that are often required in performance exams around the world. The app generates random intervals (unison to octave), chords (major, minor, dom 7, dim 7, and inversions), and cadences. Specific test requirements may be set by the user in the settings menu.

The cadence-generating algorithm can produce hundreds of unique cadences that are perfect (authentic and inauthetic), imperfect, plagal, or deceptive. The algorithm considers a large variety of SATB arrangements and keys to eliminate repetition. The app never repeats the same sequence of intervals/chords/cadences, creating a unique test for the user each time!
