Joshua Yuan

About Me

Joshua Yuan is a Canadian cellist currently based in London, UK. He holds an associate diploma (ARCT) in cello performance from Canada’s Royal Conservatory of Music.

Phil and Eli Taylor Performance Academy for Young Artists

Cello Performance (2012-2016)

Studio of David Hetherington. Recipient of the Dr. David Ouchterlony Music Scholarship, Millie Shime Memorial Scholarship, and Robert C. Smith Scholarship.

National Youth Orchestra of Canada

Cellist (2014)

Youngest member of 2014 NYO. Recorded Daphnis et Chloé.

Toronto Symphony Youth Orchestra

Cellist (2012-2016)

Principal cellist since 2014. Recipient of TSYO Leadership Award (2015, 2016).

Domaine Forget International Music and Dance Academy

Cello Performance (2013)
